The core idea

Designed to support reading, not replace it.

The best notes usually come from noticing, selecting, and deciding what matters. Send Notes keeps that step intact. Users still read the page, highlight key points, and only then ask AI to help organize those pieces into something clearer and more usable.

Intentional capture

Highlights become signals of understanding instead of raw content dumps.

Faster synthesis

AI bridges scattered snippets into structured notes, saving cleanup time.

Better recall

The act of selecting while reading helps users retain more than simple copy-paste workflows.
