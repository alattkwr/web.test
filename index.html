The core idea

Designed to support reading, not replace it.

Good notes start with paying attention, noticing what matters, and choosing what to save. Send Notes keeps that part of the process intact. You still do the reading first. The AI step comes after, helping turn your highlights into clearer, more useful notes.

Intentional capture

Highlights become signals of understanding instead of raw content dumps.

Faster organization

AI bridges scattered snippets into structured notes, saving cleanup time.

Better recall

Because you selected the material yourself, the finished notes are easier to review and remember.
